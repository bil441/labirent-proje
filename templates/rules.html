King always is born in the top-left corner. It is able to move in the main directions (left, right, up, down). Each move comes with 1 unit of cost. If king goes over the cells with lava, the cost of it is 10 unit. In case of BFS is chosen as search algorithm, lava cells are not an option to go over. Portal is a tunnel that king can be beamed up from portal entrance to portal end. It is preferable if it offers a shorter path. There may exist multiple goals (up to three). Algorithm will find the shortest path from starting to any goal.
